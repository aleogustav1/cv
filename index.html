Bio

My name is Alejandro Colón and, I am 37 years old. I was born in a little island in the Caribbean, Puerto Rico. Since I was a kid I have
been facinated with sports cars and, technology. After attempting a deegree on Graphic Arts, I decided to take a pause and
become a automotive technician. After finishing a technical degree at Mech-Tech Puerto Rico, I did a short intership at Autogermana
Puerto Rico in where at the end of that intership I applied for an open position and got hired as a MINI Cooper technician. I have always loved european
cars to begin with and, I was very excited to begin my career as a BMW/MINI technician. In 2016, I moved to Denver Colorado
where I continued my career as a MINI Cooper technician at Ralph Schomp MINI. Three more years passed and, I decided to grow
even more by getting to work for a private shop, Autoworks Colorado, who works brands like Audi, Volkswagen, BMW, MINI,
Mercedes Benz and, Porsche. Now, I find myself pursuing a career as a Web Developer.
